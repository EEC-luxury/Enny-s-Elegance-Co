Our Story

The Story Behind Enny's Elegance & Co

Enny's Elegance & Co was created to bring affordable luxury closer to everyone. We carefully source premium-quality jewelry, wristwatches, perfumes, eyewear, bags and fashion accessories that help our customers express confidence, style and elegance.

Our goal is simple: to provide beautiful products, trusted service and exceptional value without compromising quality. Whether you're shopping for yourself, preparing for a special occasion, or searching for the perfect gift — we are committed to helping you shine with confidence.

Our Mission

To make elegance accessible through premium fashion and luxury accessories.

Our Vision

To become a trusted destination for stylish and affordable luxury products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your first order with us.



💬 Ask On WhatsApp
Do you deliver nationwide?
+

Yes. We offer delivery across Nigeria, reaching customers in all major cities and states from Lagos to Abuja and beyond.

How long does delivery take?
+

Lagos and Abuja typically take 1–3 business days. Other states may take 3–7 business days depending on your location.

Are your products original?
+

We source only from trusted and verified suppliers, and we personally inspect quality before delivery. Customer satisfaction is our top priority.

How do I place an order?
+

Simply browse our store, select your items, and either add to cart or click "Order On WhatsApp" for instant assistance from our team.

Can I order as a gift?
+

Absolutely! Many of our products make excellent gifts. We offer special gift packaging and can include a personalised message on request.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept bank transfers and secure mobile payments. Full payment details are provided once you confirm your order.
